Steps to prepare for an exam

  1. Understand the exam syllabus and pattern
  2. Create a realistic study timetable
  3. Gather all required study materials
  4. Study each topic systematically
  5. Make short notes and summaries

    Required study materials

  • Textbooks and reference books
  • Class notes
  • Notebook and pen/pencil
  • Previous year question papers
  • Sample papers / mock tests
